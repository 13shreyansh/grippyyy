How It Works

1

Sequence

Navigate to the URL, capture the Accessibility Tree, and extract the form's DNA — every field, label, option, and button.

2

Classify

Identify the form species (complaint, tax, visa, registration) using keyword fingerprinting. No LLM needed.

3

Map

Match your data to form fields using a synonym dictionary. Unnamed fields get mapped by an LLM fallback with deduplication.

4

Execute

Fill every field with Playwright — text, dropdowns, radios, checkboxes, dates. Human-like delays to avoid detection.

5

Cache & Heal

Save the genome and mapping. Next time, skip steps 1-3 entirely. If the form changes, self-heal and re-learn.
