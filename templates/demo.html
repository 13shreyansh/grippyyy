How it works

1
Paste a live form URL
Use a public form that does not require login. The verified demo path is the httpbin form.
2
Grippy reads the form
The Form Genome Engine extracts every field, classifies the form type, and maps your data to each field.
3
One click to fill
Grippy opens the real website and fills the form in a browser. Some CAPTCHA flows can be handled when a solver is configured.
